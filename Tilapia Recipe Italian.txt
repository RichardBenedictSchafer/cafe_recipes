Italian Tilapia: 
Top fillets with 1 cup diced tomatoes with roasted garlic, 1/2 cup each julienned roasted sweet red pepper, sliced fresh mushrooms, diced fresh mozzarella cheese and 1/2 tsp. dried basil. 

Bake 20 min.

Nutrition Facts: 1 fillet equals 189 calories, 4 g fat (2 g saturated fat), 90 mg cholesterol, 351 mg sodium, 4 g carbohydrate, trace fiber, 34 g protein. Diabetic exchanges: 5 lean meat, 1/2 fat.
