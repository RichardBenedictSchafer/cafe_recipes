
Frikadeller:

    1 lb ground beef
    1 lb ground pork
    1 large red onion finely chopped
    3/4 cup breadcrumbs
    4 Tbsp flour
    2 eggs
    1/2 tsp salt
    1/2 tsp black pepper
    1/2 tsp nutmeg
    1/2 tsp sage 
    4 cloves of garlic minced

    half & half or milk (1/4 to 1/2 c.)
    
    Mix measured ingredients by hand 
    then add enough milk to loosen the mixture so that 
    the meatballs will flatten slightly while cooking.

    Fry in butter and olive oil over medium heat
